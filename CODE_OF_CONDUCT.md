# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, everyone participating in AudioRouterNow — through issues, pull requests, or discussions — agrees to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to [dev@mauriciomorkun.com](mailto:dev@mauriciomorkun.com).

This project adapts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
